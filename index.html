How ObiKhairat Works

List Items

Donors post gently used items they want to share with the community.

Match Requests

Our system automatically matches items with nearby requests.

Deliver

Items are delivered through meetups, community hubs, or courier.

What We Accept & How We Help

School Supplies

Backpacks, books, uniforms, shoes, and school supplies in good condition.

Children's Clothing

Clean, gently used kids' clothes for all ages and seasons.

Small Electronics

Working small electronics that can help with education and communication.

Transparent Process

Delivery-only pricing with clear breakdowns and automatic refunds for unspent shipping.

Trust & Safety

Light verification, safe meetup locations, and QR proof-of-handover for everyone's protection.

Community Impact

Reduce waste, equip students, and strengthen neighborhoods through sharing.
