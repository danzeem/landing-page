ObiKhairat:Trust, Reliable, Empathy

ObiKhairat connects gently used essentials from local donors to families in need—recipients only cover delivery. Transparent, dignified, and simple: a hand up, not a handout.

ObiKhairat at a glance

What is ObiKhairat

A trust-first bridge connecting gently used essentials from local donors to families who need them. Recipients only cover delivery—zero platform fees.

How it works

List → Match → Deliver. Donors post items, we auto-match nearby requests, and handover happens via meetup, community hub, or courier label with tracking.

What we accept

Backpacks, books, uniforms, shoes, kids’ clothes, school supplies, and small electronics in working order—clean, safe, and ready to use.

Costs & transparency

Delivery-only pricing with a clear breakdown. No hidden charges, no tipping prompts, and any unspent shipping is automatically refunded.

Trust & safety

Light verification, safe meetup locations, masked contact, QR proof-of-handover, and a simple dispute process to protect everyone.

Why it matters

Every item says “You matter.” Reduce waste, equip students, and strengthen neighborhoods—track impact by items delivered and landfill diversion.
